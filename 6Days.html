What is Included  

The package includes transport, hotel accommodation and breakfast and dinner, only. Only filtered water would be provided by the company and no bottled water. Company may also provide small quantity fresh fruit (Cherry) or dry fruit (khobani) to avoid altitude sickness, while traveling to Hunza or Khunjerab.

Any extra or additional expense including hotel room service, laundry, bottled water, Altit Baltit Fort ticket / jeep rent would be borne by the guests, separately. In case of any overstay due to land sliding (very rare) guests would have to pay additional cost.

Company reserves the right to cancel the tour due to any good reason. Full amount would be refunded if company cancels the tour.

Rawalpindi/Islamabad residents can visits the office, 5, 2nd Floor, Shamsabad, Rawalpindi, and deposit the payment, while outstation clients can secure their seat by depositing advance payment of Rs 5000 by easypaisa. They can make the remaining payment (Rs.13000) at the time of departure

Kindly send the receipt to whats app numbers after depositing money at Easypaisa account.

What to Pack- Checklist

Please pack your clothes in a backpack or rucksack. It’s easy to carry. Pack essential clothing and daily use items but keep it to minimum as you will spend each night at different places.

Please be prepared for rain and snowfall at Khunjerab and Babusar Top

Essentials

• Cotton clothing for six days

• Jacket / Rain Coat (weather at Babusar top and Khunjerab could be very cold)

• Joggers/ comfortable walking shoes and spare shoes

• Your medication

• Toiletries

While traveling from Rawalpindi to Naran, the weather changes after Kiwai and become pleasant. From Naran to Gilgit weather mostly remains very hot in summer.  

From Gilgit onwards to Hunza weather would be pleasant. In Gulmit and beyond you will experience pleasant to cold weather.

If you are traveling with kids please pack extra diapers / milk / biscuits / sweets etc
